Blockchain Network Analytics Platform

A platform providing predictive insights for blockchain networks, helping to foresee network congestion and transaction behaviors.

HTML Blockchain Analytics

1) Goal / Business Question

Monitor and predict blockchain network patterns to optimize performance and estimate gas fees accurately.

2) Data

Type: On-chain transaction logs, block propagation times, and historical gas fee records.

3) Approach (What I did)

  • Collected and cleaned historical blockchain network data.
  • Developed a frontend interface to display predictive models trained on the network data.

4) Findings

  • Identified key variables (like transaction volume surges) that consistently predict mempool congestion.

5) Deliverables

  • Predictive Insights Dashboard interface.

6) Next Steps

  • Integrate real-time WebSocket feeds from full blockchain nodes.
  • Expand the predictive model to include multiple EVM-compatible chains.
