Breathe India — Air Quality Analysis

A focused analysis to identify pollution hotspots and uncover temporal and spatial patterns for key air pollutants.

Python Pandas NumPy EDA Matplotlib Seaborn MS Excel

1) Goal / Business Question

Understand air-quality trends and determine where and when pollution risks are highest, so the insights can support research prioritization and targeted mitigation discussions.

2) Data

Type: air-quality measurements across time and locations (pollutants as key variables).

Preparation: cleaned and organized the data into analysis-ready tables; handled missing/outlier values and aligned structures for consistent EDA.

3) Approach (What I did)

  • Explored trends for key pollutants over time.
  • Studied spatial variation to surface pollution hotspots.
  • Built visual comparisons using EDA plots (time-series + distribution views).
  • Used aggregation and derived summaries to interpret pollutant behavior patterns.

4) Findings

  • Identified high-risk regions by comparing pollutant intensity across locations.
  • Revealed temporal variation, highlighting when pollution tends to peak or shift.
  • Summarized how different pollutants behave together (patterns useful for further investigation).
  • Translated analysis into clear visual reports to support decision-making and communication.
