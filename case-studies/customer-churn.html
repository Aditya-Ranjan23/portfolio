Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

Analyzing customer attrition to identify key drivers of churn using Power BI.

Power BI Data Visualization SQL

1) Goal / Business Question

Understand customer churn factors to improve retention strategies and identify high-risk customer segments.

2) Data

Type: Customer demographics, subscription details, and historical usage metrics.

3) Approach (What I did)

  • Cleaned and prepared the customer dataset for analysis.
  • Built interactive Power BI dashboards to visualize churn patterns.
  • Segmented users by risk and contract type.

4) Findings

  • Identified higher churn rates among specific contract types.
  • Revealed key indicators that precede a customer's decision to cancel.

5) Deliverables

  • Interactive Power BI Dashboard.
  • Executive summary report on retention strategies.

6) Next Steps

  • Implement predictive modeling (e.g., Logistic Regression) to forecast future churn.
  • A/B test targeted retention campaigns.
